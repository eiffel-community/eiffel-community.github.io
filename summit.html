Eiffel Summit 2022.1

This in-person two-day conference for people interested in the Eiffel protocol and its ecosystem will have a mix of presentations and discussions. Newcomers as well as experienced practitioners are all welcome.

Attendance is free of charge but to ease the planning of the event we ask that you register no later than September 30, 2022.

If you're interested in presenting something yourself please reach out to eiffel-tc@googlegroups.com no later than September 16, 2022.
