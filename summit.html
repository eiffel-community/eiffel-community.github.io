Eiffel Summit 2023.1

This in-person two-day conference for people interested in the Eiffel protocol and its ecosystem will have a mix of presentations and discussions. Newcomers as well as experienced practitioners are welcome.

The practicalities

  • Where: doWhile, Lindholmspiren 7, 417 56 Gothenborg, Sweden
  • When: June 13, 09:00 CEST, 2023, to June 14, 17:00 CEST (exact start and end times tentative)
  • Who: To get in touch with the organizers send an email to eiffel-tc@googlegroups.com.
  • Communication: For questions, comments, or suggestions regarding the Summit, please join us on Slack.

Agenda

At this early stage very little has been decided regarding the agenda. This page will be updated as we get closer to the date.

That said, the second day, June 14, is adjacent to the Software Center reporting workshop, and the afternoon of this day aims to cater to a wider audience of people who may not be familar with Eiffel in particular but is well-versed in the CI/CD space in general, possibly from the academic side.

If there's any topic you'd like to see covered please reach out to us.
